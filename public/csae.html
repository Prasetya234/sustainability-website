**Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) Policy**

Effective Date: 28 February 2025


At GBVH, we are committed to maintaining a safe and secure environment for all users. We have a zero-tolerance policy toward Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) and strictly enforce measures to prevent, detect, and remove any content or behavior that violates our standards.

### 1. **Prohibited Content and Activities**
We strictly prohibit:
- Any form of child sexual abuse material (CSAM).
- Grooming, coercion, or any activity that exploits minors.
- The sharing, solicitation, or promotion of CSAE-related content.

### 2. **Detection and Reporting Mechanisms**
We employ:
- Automated detection systems to identify and remove CSAE content.
- Dedicated moderation teams to review and take immediate action.
- User reporting tools to allow the community to report suspicious behavior.

All reports are promptly reviewed, and appropriate actions—including account suspension and law enforcement notification—are taken as necessary.

### 3. **Collaboration with Authorities**
We work closely with:
- Law enforcement agencies to report and act against CSAE violations.
- Child protection organizations to enhance our safety policies.
- Industry coalitions to implement best practices in CSAE prevention.

### 4. **User Responsibilities**
Users must:
- Refrain from engaging in any activity that violates our CSAE policy.
- Report any suspected CSAE content or behavior immediately.
- Comply with all applicable laws and our community guidelines.

### 5. **Enforcement Actions**
Violations of this policy will result in:
- Immediate removal of offending content.
- Account suspension or permanent banning.
- Reporting to appropriate law enforcement agencies.

### 6. **Contact and Reporting**
If you encounter any CSAE-related content on our platform, please report it immediately through contact us at mitramandiritech.smg@gmail.com.
