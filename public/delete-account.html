🗑️ How to Request Account Deletion

If you need to delete your account from our internal company application, please follow the steps below. For security and compliance reasons, account deletion cannot be performed directly by users and must be requested through the proper channel.

🔐 Step-by-Step Guide

1. Prepare Your Request

  • Your full name
  • Your employee ID
  • Your company email address
  • Reason for requesting account deletion (optional but recommended)

2. Send a Request Email

Use your company email to send a request to: support@ontidecorp.com

Email Subject: Account Deletion Request – [Your Full Name]

Subject: Account Deletion Request – John Doe Dear Support Team, I would like to request the deletion of my account from the internal company application. Here are my details: - Full Name: John Doe - Employee ID: 123456 - Email: john.doe@companydomain.com - Reason: [Optional: e.g., Leaving the company] Please confirm once the deletion has been processed or if you need any further information from me. Thank you, John Doe

3. Wait for Confirmation

The support team will process your request and confirm once your account has been successfully deleted. Please allow up to 3 business days for processing.

⚠️ Important Notes

  • Once your account is deleted, all associated data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.
  • This action is irreversible.
  • If you're leaving the company, account deletion will be handled as part of the offboarding process.
